# CRM Value Library content brief

The library should help a contractor send something genuinely useful instead of another “just checking in” email. The upload/link workflow is built; the final resources should be created only after product direction is approved.

## First proposed resource set

### 1. Bid Decision Readiness Checklist

- **Audience:** owner, developer, or architect before a bid decision
- **Job:** expose the decisions, allowances, alternates, and approval dates that can delay award or mobilization
- **Likely format:** branded one-page PDF plus an editable worksheet

### 2. Owner Decision Map

- **Audience:** owner representative and project leadership during handoff
- **Job:** make cost, scope, design, and schedule authority explicit
- **Likely format:** branded one-page map with decision types, owners, and response targets

### 3. First 30 Days Project Roadmap

- **Audience:** newly awarded client and internal delivery team
- **Job:** show kickoff, submittals, long-lead releases, risk review, billing setup, and communication cadence
- **Likely format:** visual timeline plus an editable planning page

## Direction needed before production

For each resource, decide:

- whether it is external/client-facing, internal, or both;
- the intended contractor type and project size;
- the exact ALP/OverWatch voice and call to action;
- whether the asset should be a PDF, editable template, short video, or combination;
- whether Harbor Residence should include a completed example, a blank template, or both.

Once those choices are made, the assets can be authored, added to the Harbor walkthrough, and marked approved for external sharing.
